Poem · Renyjulita

Renyjulita

you once were a little sun in a dim morning,

greeting everyone with warmth,

hiding your own fatigue behind a gentle smile.

you listened to the world’s sorrow,

even when your own voice was lost in the noise.

you lit candles to guide others’ paths,

while your own feet wandered in the dark.

but time has taught you something —

not every hand you help will hold you back with honesty.

sometimes, giving too much makes you disappear.

now, Renyjulita has changed —

she looks into the mirror, not to find her scars,

but to recognize who she truly is.

she tends to herself, not out of pride,

but because she finally knows: she, too, deserves to be cared for.
